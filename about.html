About

The *ANFX Film Festival* is an annual film festival celebrating animation and visual effects in Aotearoa New Zealand, alongside a sample of international animation.

The festival showcases the very best of short form animation and VFX in Aotearoa, and focuses especially on young creators, experimental forms, Māori voices and emerging approaches.

Each year the 2 day festival features a roster of the best local animated and VFX short films, screened at Wellington venues, and includes discussions and interviews with directors and creators. Each year also includes an international sampler or featured guest.

The festival aims to stimulate new work and bring together animators, creators, directors and teams from across NZ. We exist to foster community, create support systems and networking opportunities and provide a great platform to showcase and celebrate animation and VFX.
